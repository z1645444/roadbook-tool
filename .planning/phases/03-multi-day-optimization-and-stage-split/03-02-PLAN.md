  <action>Create `splitRouteIntoDayStages(segments, constraints)` that:
1) computes `dailyMaxRideMinutes = min(intensity.caps.rideMinutes, rideWindow.minutes)`,
2) appends segments to current day until next segment would exceed daily max, then starts new day,
3) sets each day's `startPoint` from first segment `from`, `endPoint` from last segment `to`,
4) sets `overnightStopPoint` to `endPoint` for all days except final arrival day,

the analog mapped in `03-PATTERNS.md`.</action>
